Welcome

I'm Sam Burer, Tippie Rollins Professor in the Department of Business Analytics at the University of Iowa. My research and teaching focus on analytics, operations research, management sciences, and optimization.

I received my Ph.D. in Algorithms, Combinatorics, and Optimization from Georgia Tech. My work has been supported by the National Science Foundation, and I currently serve on the editorial boards of Operations Research and Mathematical Programming. I have also served on the editorial boards of Management Science, SIAM Journal on Optimization, and Mathematics of Operations Research.

About

Research Areas

My research focuses on the theory and computational methods for nonconvex and convex optimization, with particular emphasis on discrete and continuous optimization, semidefinite programming, copositive programming, and decision-making under uncertainty. I am passionate about developing new mathematical formulations and algorithms for computationally challenging problems.
