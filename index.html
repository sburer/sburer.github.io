Welcome

I'm Sam Burer, Tippie Rollins Professor in the Department of Business Analytics at the University of Iowa. My research and teaching focus on analytics, operations research, management sciences, and optimization.

I received my Ph.D. in Algorithms, Combinatorics, and Optimization from Georgia Tech. My work has been supported by the National Science Foundation, and I serve on the editorial boards of Operations Research, SIAM Journal on Optimization, Mathematics of Operations Research, Management Science, and Optima.

About My Work

I'm passionate about solving complex optimization problems and helping students develop strong analytical skills. My research interests span convex optimization, machine learning (from an optimization perspective), semidefinite programming, and copositive programming.

I've had the privilege of working with talented students and collaborators from around the world. Some highlights:

  • Council Member of the Mathematical Optimization Society
  • Board of Directors Member of the INFORMS Computing Society
  • 2020 INFORMS Computing Society Prize winner
  • 4,800+ citations and 80+ publications
